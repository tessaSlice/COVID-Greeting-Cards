Worldwide: Who Are We?

We student-led organization based in Michigan designing and delivering personalized digital greeting cards. All profit is donated towards COVID causes -- so you can make a difference while making a loved one's day by purchasing one of our greeting cards.

Our Goal:

The money collected from the cards will be used to help those impacted by COVID. We will donate our proceeds to Midland Open Door.

How to Purchase a Greeting Card:

Simply click on the button located at the top of the website or go to the menu and click on ‘Purchase Here’. It’ll take you to a google form where you can fill out with your email and purchase through PayPal.

Why Us?

  • Profit donated to a nonprofit: you can make a difference!
  • Digital greeting cards are eco-friendly!
  • You can email a card to someone halfway around the world
  • They are quick and efficient
  • We do commissions - if you have any specific requests for how you want your greeting card to look like, we can make it for you
  • Can’t lose them! We will provide you a digital PDF version of your card in case you wish to use it again.
